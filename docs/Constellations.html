Constellations

Constellations is an immersive puzzle game, where the player explores a temple island in a realistic environment to reveal a narrative through puzzle based gameplay and discover the mystery of the stars.

During my major production at AIE, my team and I got to create a puzzle-based narrative game based around Greek mythology and its connections with the stars. From this project I learnt a lot about not only creating puzzle-based gameplay mechanics but also working in a team and all the setbacks or challenges that arise in game development.

Pisces & Sagittarius Puzzle

These two out of the four puzzles were my responsibility during the project. The Sagittarius puzzle on the left I created the movement behind the mirrors that the player uses to direct a beam of light to a brazier. With Pisces puzzle on the right, I used similar movement to the mirrors. However, I added the ability to configure each channel to rotate with other channels at different degrees. Additionally I had to implement a water effect to simulate the channels filling up with water as they are completed.
